More-Section Help

Unsure on how use the features in the 'More' section? Take a look below.

Experiments

  1. First, tap the dashboard_customize icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap on any listed experiment, e.g. 'Social'.

  3. Try out the experiment as you please.

About and More

  1. First, tap the dashboard_customizez icon on the bottom navigation bar.

  2. Tap on whichever you want to use, 'About' or 'Terms & Conditions'.

  3. Look at the information on the pages.
