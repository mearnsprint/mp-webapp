WiFi and Internet Help


Need help on how to use/connect to the internet* at our branches? Please read the documentation below:
  1. First, open the Settings app from your launcher.

  2. Then, click on WiFi or Network and Internet**.

  3. Click on the WiFi network called The Highland Cafe™️ WiFi and follow steps to create/sign into your THCAccount details to connect!

*WiFi-enabled device required.
**WiFi settings' location depends on brand of phone you use.
